# iNCHWRM
![iNCHWRM Logo](/iNCHWRM.png)
An impractical, Framework based custom laptop project, measured in inches. 

## The Concept 
The idea is to create a custom laptop that meets my impossibly specific desires. 
Those desires are as follows, 
- A mechanical keyboard utilizing both full size switches and keycaps
- No built in microphone
- No built in camera
- No trackpad
- Hot swappable i/o
- A satisfying way to turn the beast on and off

and lastly, 

- unapologetically thick

## The Method 
For the bulk majority of the projects components i will be utilizing the various components built for the Framework 13. For the brains, I have selected the AMD Ryzen 5 7640U mainboard as i do not anticipate needing the power of the Ryzen 7. This unlikely, however, potential oversight can easily be corrected on a $500 whim, as is the nature of Frameworks ecosystem.
